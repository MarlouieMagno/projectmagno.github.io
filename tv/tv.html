TITANS (2018-2023)

created by Akiva Goldsman, Geoff Johns, and Greg Berlanti

           Titans is a live-action superhero television series developed by Akiva Goldsman, Geoff Johns, and Greg Berlanti. The show is based on the DC Comics superhero team known as the Teen Titans, and follows the adventures of a group of young heroes as they fight crime and confront their personal demons. The series begins with the character of Dick Grayson, also known as Robin, who has left his life as Batman's sidekick and is working as a detective in Detroit. He meets Rachel Roth, a young girl with mysterious powers who is being pursued by a dangerous organization known as the Cult of the Raven. Together, they embark on a journey to discover the truth about Rachel's past and her connection to the demonic entity known as Trigon. As the series progresses, other members of the Teen Titans are introduced, including Kory Anders (Starfire), a powerful alien with no memory of her past, and Garfield Logan (Beast Boy), a shapeshifter who joins the team after being rescued from a criminal organization.

The first family consists of Jay, his second wife Gloria, and her young son Manny. The second family is Jay's daughter Claire, her husband Phil, and their three children. The third family is Jay's son Mitchell, his partner Cameron, and their adopted daughter Lily. created by Tom Kapinos

The show is based on the DC Comics character Lucifer Morningstar, who is portrayed as a charming and charismatic devil who has abandoned his throne in Hell and moved to Los Angeles to run a nightclub. The series follows Lucifer as he teams up with LAPD detective Chloe Decker to solve crimes in Los Angeles. Along the way, Lucifer grapples with his own morality and identity, as well as his complicated relationships with his family members and celestial beings. The show is known for its blend of procedural crime-solving and supernatural elements, as well as its exploration of themes such as redemption, free will, and the nature of good and evil. The series features a diverse cast of characters, including Mazikeen, a demon who serves as Lucifer's ally and enforcer, and Amenadiel, an angel who serves as Lucifer's adversary and brother.
