Tokyo, Japan

One of the main draws of Tokyo is its unique blend of modern and traditional elements. On one hand, you can marvel at the futuristic architecture of buildings like the Tokyo Skytree and the Tokyo Tower. On the other hand, you can also explore historic sites like the Meiji Shrine and the Senso-ji Temple. In addition to its rich cultural offerings, Tokyo is also known for its incredible food scene. Whether you're looking to sample sushi, ramen, or other Japanese delicacies, there are endless options to choose from.
